About Us

We’re more than just a club – we’re a coding family!

Hey there, future Byte Buster! Wondering who we are and what we do? Well, sit back and scroll on because you're about to find out.

Our History

The Byte Buster Coding Club came to life 3 years ago when a bunch of us realised that school wasn't satisfying our appetite for coding. Since then, we've grown into a tight-knit community that eats, sleeps, and breathes all things digital.

Our Mission

To fuel our passion for coding and digital creation, to help each other grow as coders, and most importantly, to have a ton of fun while doing it.
